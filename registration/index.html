Join ACM

Join ACM SIGAPP

  • Professional (SIG Member yearly Rate:$15.00)

  • Student (SIG Member yearly Rate:$8.00)

  • ACM Member or ACM SigAPP member rates are only available to individuals who are ACM or ACM SigAPP members at the time of registration.

  • ACM Member or ACM SigAPP members must input their ACM member or ACM SigAPP member id at the time of registration and show their valid ACM or ACM SigAPP membership card at the Conference registration Desk.

  • The Student Registration is applicable to University/College Students only. Pre-Registered Students and those registering on site must show a current STUDENT ID upon checking in at the ICEA 2020.

Registration Policy

  • Each Accepted Poster Paper at ICEA 2020 must have at least one author registered with the Poster Author rate at the camera ready submission deadline of July 31.

  • Each Accepted Regular registration can cover 1 Regular Paper.

  • ACM Member or ACM SigAPP member rates are only available to individuals who are ACM or ACM SigAPP members at the time of registration.

Registration Type

An author must register as Paper Author. Please note that a Student Registration does NOT cover paper inclusion in the conference proceeding. Student registration is intended to encourage student attendance.
